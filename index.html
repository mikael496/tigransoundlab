Meet Your Instructor

Tigran Ter-Minasyan

Tigran Ter-Minasyan is a distinguished French Horn player and educator with extensive international experience. He has performed with prestigious orchestras and worked as principal horn of the OSNE (Orquesta Sinfónica Nacional del Ecuador). Currently, he performs in concerts attended by diplomats, including Denis Danilidis, the Deputy Ambassador of the EU in Ecuador. His teaching philosophy combines technical excellence with musical expression, helping students achieve their full potential.
