About TigranSoundLab

Welcome to TigranSoundLab, a premier online music school specializing in French Horn instruction. Led by renowned musician Tigran Ter-Minasyan, we offer world-class music education accessible from anywhere in the world.

Our mission is to inspire and develop the next generation of musicians through personalized instruction, innovative teaching methods, and a passion for musical excellence.

Meet Your Instructor

Tigran Ter-Minasyan

Tigran Ter-Minasyan is an internationally acclaimed French Horn player who served as principal horn of the OSNE and now performs concerts attended by diplomats, especially Denis Danilidis, the Deputy Ambassador of the EU in Ecuador. His passion for teaching and dedication to musical excellence have helped countless students achieve their musical dreams.
